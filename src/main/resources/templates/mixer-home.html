Welcome to the JobCoin Mixer!

  1. Add a list of the addresses you want your coins to be transferred to after mixing
  2. Add an optional email address to prevent accidentally receiving your own coins back
  3. Click "Get Deposit Address" to receive a deposit address
  4. Send the amount you wish to mix to the Deposit Address (Must be a single deposit!)
  5. Receive your coins at the provided addresses within the specified timeframe
